

Do's

* Think before you post-ensure content is respectful and
  appropriate.
* Maintain privacy-adjust settings and protect personal information.
* Use social media for learning and networking.
* Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news).
* Be respectful in comments and interactions.
* Build a positive digital footprint for future opportunities.
* Balance online and offline life.


Don'ts

* Don't share offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
* Don't share sensitive details like address, phone number, or passwords.
* Don't waste excessive time scrolling or procrastinating.
* Don't spread rumors, misinformation, or unverified content.
* Don't engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or arguments.
* Don't post content that could harm your reputation.
* Don't let social media affect your studies or mental well-being.
